About Me

I’ve always been into tech, not just using it but figuring out how it all works. Whether I’m editing a short film or setting up some new gear, I enjoy learning by diving in.
I studied film at uni, mostly focusing on editing and post-production. Over time, I got more interested in the tech behind the tools I was using, wondering how they worked and how they could be better. That curiosity led me into web development, and I found a new kind of creative problem-solving that really stuck.
I like figuring out how things fit together, whether it's code, design, or everyday tools. I'm especially drawn to tech that helps people create or connect in cool ways.
In my downtime, I’m usually watching movies (and probably overthinking them), working out, or doing a bit of bouldering. I’m into anything that’s hands-on, a bit challenging, and actually fun.
